about us

We're not your average coffee house.

Like the vigilante heroes in our beloved comic books, we saw the injustice in our local cafe scene. The sky-high prices. The limited coffee options. The lack of comfy couches. So we took matters into our own hands and Vigilatte Coffee House was born.
